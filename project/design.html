Inforgrpahic Resume

The Task

The final project for a multimedia design course that I took was to design my resume in a creative way.

The Idea

Turn my resume into an inforgraphic. I realized I had developed a diverse skill set through experience across several different fields. I wanted to showcase these skills in a visual way. The darkened part of each orange ring represents my proficiency and experience in that particular field.
