Rivalry Week

The Task

Many of the top college football programs in the country had begun producing hype videos prior to facing their top rival for use on social media. Senior officials at Miami along with the football coaching staff had taken notice to this and asked the digital strategy deparmtent if we could produce something similar in the week leading up to our largest rivlary game against Florida State. The Associate AD of Marketing and Digital Strategy assigned me this task.

The Idea

My idea was simple: create a highlight package timed to action movie trailer music. We had not released a highlight package through the first three months of the season, so my thought was to recap the awesome moments of the season thus far and get fans excited for the game against Florida State.

BUC Goin' Up

The Task

Create a video that will get people excited about an important matchup against No. 12 Louisville and encourage them to come to the game.

The Idea

My idea was to create something that was witty and inline with pop culture that would get shared amongst fans. Earlier that season, we had a Tuesday home game and I created a highlight video with the popular rap song Tuesday by ILOVEMAKONNEN featuring Drake as the soundtrack. The idea for this video was to replicate that theme, but put a new spin on it. With help from the Marketing department, I was able to get our star point guard, Angel Rodriguez, to sing "Got the BUC going up on a Tuesday." I added in highlights from previous home games that season where there had been a large crowd. I wanted to incorporate another element to really make the video stand out, so I went through our head coach's press conference videos looking for specific words. I editied them together into the video below. Additionally, the most popular part of the video was posted on Vine.
